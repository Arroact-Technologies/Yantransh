About Nalmani

Nalmani, Precision in Every Turn

Nalmani by Yantransh is a premium range of ceramic diverter cartridges and faucet components engineered for smooth operation, leak-free performance, and lasting durability. Built with high-grade ceramic discs and reinforced brass stems, Nalmani ensures reliable flow control under high pressure and temperature. Perfect for residential and commercial fittings, Nalmani combines innovation, precision, and endurance — the core of Yantransh quality.
