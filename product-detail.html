Quick guide to installation and performance

YV31047055 Plastic Diverter Cartridge Installation

Step 1 : Remove the Old Cartridge

Begin by turning off the main water supply to prevent any accidental leakage. Using a wrench or Allen key, gently unscrew and lift the faucet handle. Once removed, you’ll see the old cartridge seated inside the faucet body. Grip it carefully and pull it out with a steady motion.

YV31047055 Plastic Diverter Cartridge Installation

Step 2 : Clean the Housing

Inspect the faucet housing and clean any mineral deposits or debris. Use a soft cloth to wipe the inner surfaces to ensure a smooth fit for the new cartridge.

YV31047055 Plastic Diverter Cartridge Installation

Step 3 : Insert New Cartridge

Align the new cartridge with the grooves inside the faucet and push it in firmly until seated. Make sure orientation pins are matched correctly to avoid leaks.

Step 4 : Reassemble & Test

Reattach the handle and tighten the fixing screw. Turn on the main water supply and test for smooth operation and drip‑free performance.
